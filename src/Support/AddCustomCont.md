# Adding Custom Content To The Editor

### Size:
- The default editor size is approximately `528x235`.
    - It's recommended to use an image with these dimensions to avoid scaling issues.

---

## Current Interface:

1. Open the [Interface Directory](InterfaceDir.md) and open the `Monaco` folder.
2. Open `Monaco.html` with `Notepad`.
3. Replace `img_url` with your content URL.
