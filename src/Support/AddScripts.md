## Legacy Interface:
- Open up your Krnl directory, then find the `scripts` folder.
    - Open the `scripts` folder.

<p align="center">
    <img src="https://i.imgur.com/1v5ag4P.png">
</p>

- In the folder, create a new Text Document.
    - Both `.txt` and `.lua` file formats are supported.
